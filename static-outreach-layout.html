Outreach Materials

Calisphere is visited a million times a year by undergraduates, K-12 students, teachers, professors, genealogists, artists, and other curious people for their personal and professional projects and endeavors.

Help us spread the word about this great resource to your patrons, friends, and colleagues.

Social media tools

Items on Calisphere can be easily shared on social media. On each item, look for the Twitter and Facebook buttons. Send it to your followers with a single click.

Embeddable banners
